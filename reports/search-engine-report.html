What is a <meta /> tag?
Meta tags are summarized text that lets the search engine know what the site will be about
What is the purpose for Keyword and Description meta tags?
Keyword and Description meta tags help the page by adding a summary of what the site is about under the page, meta descriptions impact a site's click-through-rate that impacts the ranking of the page
Why might you include meta tags in your pages?
meta tags are important to help impact your sites click-through-rate, most people will be more inclined to click on your website and help with rating and traffic.
Do meta tags make your pages more easily searchable or improve its ranking?
Meta tags help do both, adding meta tags will help your site more searchable and will let the user know more about your site without clicking on it yet and when done will they will be more inclined to click which helps with search ranking and the engine will boost it more higher
