What Does Supporting A Panda Do?

  • Supporting a panda by donating can help researchers equip more cameras to help furthur record their movements when they're out and about
  • You can donate to the natural reserve to help maintain the pandas in a protected environment
  • Simply by adopting a panda you help the panda reserve the resources it needs to keep it's species alive

How To Support Pandas

Pandas one of the most recognizable adorable furry creature have been an icon around the world, they were once used as diplomatic gifts and are now on the brink of extinction, these pandas must be saved and here how you can make a diffrence for them.

  • Support them by visitng here and donating to the organization
  • By adopting a panda you can recieve monthly updates on the panda of your choosing and get extra goodies including a photo of your panda.
